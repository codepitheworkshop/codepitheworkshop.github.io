About Code pi

Code pi aims at delivering industry centric workshop to students, with a full hands-on sessions on Internet of things with world's most popular single board computer Raspberry pi, the top language of 21st century Python and also deals with databases

Objectives

  • Understanding the concept of Raspberry pi Programming and it will enable them to program codes on their own
  • Acquiring knowledge on IoT and its applications.
  • Acquiring knowledge on application of Microcontrollers in various real time systems and Internet of Things
  • Generating ideas for future projects

Instructions and Rules

  • Students are expected to register as a team comprising of 6 members (max in a team)
  • A kit will be provided to a team
  • Each team is expected to bring at least one laptop for software installation
  • The registration amount is Rs.3600 per team
  • Workshop will be conducted for 1 day
